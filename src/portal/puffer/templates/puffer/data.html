Puffer Data


Along with our research paper, we are publishing an archive of anonymized data collected on Puffer for the research community to investigate. The data format is described in Appendix B of the paper. We will be happy to provide clarification and more details regarding the data in our Google Group; please don't hesitate to post a question.

We encourage you to download a small sample of fake data first to determine if the huge amount of full data is actually what you need. We would also appreciate it if you could download the full data only once. Below is the list of URLs to download each month of Puffer data; the JSON file is for looking up experimental setting. New data and results will be posted weekly; please stand by!
